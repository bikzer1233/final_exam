🐾 Palworld – Survival + Creature‑Tamer Hybrid Guide

🌍 Game Overview

🛠 Core Gameplay Tips

  • Build early base structures like Ranches, Berry Farms, and Palboxes to automate resource generation :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.
  • Capture Pals instead of killing them to gain more experience and unlock missions faster :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}.
  • Upgrade health, weight, and stamina stats early for better carry capacity and mobility :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}.

👥 Pal Management & Base Strategy

  • Assign task‑suited Pals (e.g. mining, planting, water) to appropriate stations to maximize productivity :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}.
  • Keep Pals fed and their sanity high—unhappy Pals refuse tasks and slow output :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}.
  • Avoid overusing rare technology points; prioritize tools and mounts that enhance exploration and base building :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}.

⚔️ Combat & Raid Tips

  • During raids, summon stronger combat Pals rather than worker Pals to defend your base :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}.
  • The Seed Mine skill is highly effective for boss fights; breed Pals that can learn it early :contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}.
