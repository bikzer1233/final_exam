💣 Counter-Strike: Global Offensive – Complete Gamer’s Guide

📘 Game Overview

CS:GO is a tactical first-person shooter where two teams—Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists—compete to either plant/defuse bombs or eliminate the opposing team. Developed by Valve and Hidden Path Entertainment, it’s one of the most competitive shooters in eSports history.

🎯 Weapon Essentials

  • AK-47 – One-shot headshot, used by Terrorists.
  • M4A1-S / M4A4 – Counter-Terrorist rifles with different spray patterns.
  • AWP – High-damage sniper rifle, lethal in skilled hands.
  • Desert Eagle (Deagle) – Powerful pistol with high risk-reward gameplay.

📍 Map Knowledge

Knowing your maps is critical. Here are the most played maps and their basic strategies:

  • Mirage – Great for mid-control and aggressive A-site plays.
  • Inferno – Banana control is key. Use utility to win early duels.
  • Dust II – Iconic map. A long, mid, and B tunnel are constant pressure points.

🎓 Pro Tips for Beginners

  • Adjust your sensitivity: most pros use low DPI + low in-game sensitivity.
  • Train spray control with AK and M4 in aim maps.
  • Learn pop flash and smoke spots to help your team take control.
  • Stay calm during clutch situations. Sound and timing win rounds.
  • Watch pro demos to learn common strategies and rotations.

📺 Bonus: Recommended Settings

  • Resolution: 1280x960 (Stretched)
  • Crosshair: Static style, color cyan or green
  • Radar: Rotate and zoom enabled
  • Sound: Use stereo headphones, enable HRTF
