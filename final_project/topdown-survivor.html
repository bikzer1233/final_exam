🧟 Top-Down Survivor – Survive the Horde

📜 Overview

*Top-Down Survivor* is a fast-paced survival game where you must outlast waves of relentless enemies. Equipped with limited ammo and special abilities, your goal is to survive as long as possible while upgrading your arsenal and unlocking new areas.

🎮 Core Mechanics

  • 🕹️ WASD or arrow keys for movement, mouse to aim/shoot
  • 💥 Power-ups like health kits, rapid fire, and shields
  • ⚔️ Enemies increase in number and difficulty over time
  • 🔄 Randomized loot and procedural level expansion
  • 📈 Score system based on kills and survival time

🧟 Enemy Types

  • Walkers – Basic slow enemies
  • Runners – Fast and weak, but dangerous in groups
  • Exploders – Detonate on death, dealing AOE damage
  • Bosses – Appear after every 5 waves with unique attacks

💡 Survival Tips

  • 📦 Always grab loot early between waves
  • 🔄 Use dash or blink ability to escape corners
  • 🧠 Conserve ammo — melee when possible
  • 🏃 Move in circles to kite enemies and reduce hits
  • ⚙️ Upgrade reload speed and max HP early
