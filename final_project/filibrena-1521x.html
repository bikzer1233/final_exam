🔫 Filibrena 1521X – Gun-Mage War in Philippine Fantasy

📜 Overview

*Filibrena 1521X* is a fantasy shooter-RPG inspired by Philippine myth and colonial history. You play as a "Gun Mage" resisting dark forces during an alternate version of the 1521 conquest, where magic and futuristic technology collide.

⚙️ Game Mechanics

  • 🔫 One-shot kill gunplay with limited ammo based on "Love Power"
  • ✨ NPCs drain or refill love power depending on interactions
  • 🔄 Defeating enemies regenerates your power — killing innocents weakens you
  • 🚷 Stealth required in historical towns to avoid unwanted attention

🧙 Characters

  • Laon – A rebel mage who believes in the return of Bathala
  • Cruz – A Spanish gun priest who uses divine bullets
  • Binhi – A half-diwata scout who can cloak and teleport

💡 Developer Tips

  • 🕹️ Balance gun cooldown with magic buffs to keep players active
  • 🎨 Use Filipino lore like diwata, kapre, and manananggal for bosses
  • 🎭 Consider moral choices for power-ups (save vs. kill NPCs)
  • 🎶 Use traditional instruments (kulintang, agong) for soundtrack
