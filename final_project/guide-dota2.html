⚔️ Dota 2 – Beginner’s MOBA Guide

📘 Overview

Dota 2 is a competitive 5v5 online battle arena developed by Valve. It is known for its complexity, deep strategy, and large hero pool. Players must work together to destroy the enemy team’s Ancient while protecting their own.

👥 Roles & Lanes

  • Safe Lane Carry – Farms gold and carries late-game (e.g., Juggernaut, Phantom Assassin)
  • Midlane – High-damage casters or gankers (e.g., Storm Spirit, Tinker)
  • Offlane – Tanky or disruptive heroes (e.g., Tidehunter, Underlord)
  • Hard Support – Wards and protects team (e.g., Crystal Maiden, Lion)
  • Soft Support – Roams, sets up kills (e.g., Earth Spirit, Mirana)

📍 Map Knowledge

The Dota 2 map is split diagonally by the river. Each side has 3 lanes: Safe lane, Mid lane, and Offlane. The jungle contains neutral camps for farming and objectives like Roshan.

💡 Beginner Tips

  • Always buy wards and place them for vision.
  • Learn the basics of last-hitting and denying creeps.
  • Stick with your team in mid and late game.
  • Check the minimap constantly for ganks or fights.
  • Practice one role until you're confident with it.
