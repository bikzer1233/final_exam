🌌 Genshin Impact – Beginner’s Adventure Guide

🌍 Overview

Genshin Impact is an open-world action RPG by HoYoverse, featuring elemental combat, exploration, and gacha-based character acquisition. Travel across Teyvat, solve puzzles, defeat bosses, and build the ultimate team.

🌀 Elemental Reactions

  • Vaporize (Hydro + Pyro): Massive single-target damage
  • Overloaded (Pyro + Electro): AoE blast damage
  • Frozen (Cryo + Hydro): Freezes enemies in place
  • Superconduct (Electro + Cryo): Defense reduction

🧝 Top Characters to Start

  • Noelle – Defense-based healer/tank (great for beginners)
  • Kaeya – Cryo sword user, good early game DPS
  • Amber – Pyro archer used for puzzles and early Pyro access
  • Barbara – Hydro healer, easy to get after Adventure Rank 20

🗺️ Regions of Teyvat

Genshin Impact currently features these major regions:

  • Mondstadt – Wind/Anemo nation, early quests and exploration
  • Liyue – Geo region, full of treasures and contracts
  • Inazuma – Electro-focused islands under strict rule
  • Sumeru – Nature and Dendro-themed forest/city region

🔧 Beginner Tips

  • Save Primogems for limited-time banners, not standard wishes.
  • Use Resin wisely to farm weekly bosses and domains.
  • Level artifacts only at Adventure Rank 40+.
  • Elemental synergy is more important than pure damage early on.
