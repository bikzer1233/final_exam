🔫 PUBG – Battleground Survival Guide

📘 Overview

PlayerUnknown’s Battlegrounds (PUBG) is a battle royale game where 100 players fight to be the last person or squad standing. You'll parachute onto an island, scavenge for weapons, and stay inside the shrinking safe zone.

🧭 Game Modes

  • Solo – Everyone for themselves
  • Duo – You and one partner
  • Squad – Teams of 4
  • Ranked – Competitive play with skill-based matchmaking

🔫 Weapons & Gear

PUBG offers a variety of guns, scopes, grenades, and armor. Learn the recoil patterns and effective ranges of each.

  • ARs – M416, AKM, SCAR-L
  • DMRs – SKS, SLR, Mini 14
  • Snipers – Kar98k, AWM
  • SMGs – UMP45, Vector
  • Utility – Smoke grenades, Frag grenades

🗺️ Popular Maps

  • Erangel – Classic map with forests, fields, and towns
  • Miramar – Desert map with long-range combat
  • Sanhok – Small and fast-paced jungle map
  • Vikendi – Snow-covered map with moderate pacing

💡 Beginner Tips

  • Land in less crowded areas to gear up safely.
  • Use cover — don’t run in open fields without a plan.
  • Control recoil and use burst fire.
  • Keep an eye on the circle and move early.
  • Smoke grenades are essential for revives and rotations.
