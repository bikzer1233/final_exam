🧙‍♂️ League of Legends - Beginner’s Guide

📘 Overview

League of Legends (LoL) is a 5v5 multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) where two teams fight to destroy the other team’s Nexus. Choose from 160+ champions, each with unique abilities and roles.

🏹 Champion Roles

  • Top Lane – Tanky bruisers (e.g. Garen, Darius)
  • Jungle – Roam between lanes to support (e.g. Master Yi, Amumu)
  • Mid Lane – Mages and assassins (e.g. Ahri, Zed)
  • ADC (Bot) – Ranged damage (e.g. Jinx, Caitlyn)
  • Support (Bot) – Protect and enable ADC (e.g. Thresh, Leona)

🎯 Beginner Tips

  • Always last hit minions for gold (CS).
  • Don’t overextend without vision control.
  • Stick with your team in mid-to-late game.
  • Ping to communicate rather than typing too much.
  • Start with beginner-friendly champions like Garen, Annie, or Ashe.

🌍 Map Knowledge

The main map is Summoner’s Rift. Know the three lanes, jungle camps, and objectives:

  • Baron Nashor – Powerful buff for late-game pushes
  • Dragons – Provide team-wide bonuses
  • Turrets & Inhibitors – Defend the base
