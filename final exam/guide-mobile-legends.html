📱 Mobile Legends: Bang Bang – Gamer's Guide

🌟 Overview

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) is a popular 5v5 mobile MOBA made for quick matches and strategic team play, especially in Southeast Asia.

🔰 Role Breakdown

  • Tank/Support – Initiates and protects (e.g., Tigreal, Angela)
  • Fighter – Durable damage dealers (e.g., Chou, Aldous)
  • Assassin – Burst flankers (e.g., Gusion, Lancelot)
  • Marksman – Late-game damage (e.g., Karrie, Claude)
  • Mage – Area spells and burst (e.g., Lunox, Valir)

💡 Beginner Tips

  • Focus on last‐hitting minions for gold important for Marksman gameplay.
  • Wards and vision make a massive difference—use them smartly.
  • Use hero combos: tank + carry works well.
  • Join team fights at dragon or turtle objectives.
  • Don’t overextend alone—stick with your team.
