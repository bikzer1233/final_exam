🎯 Valorant - Complete Beginner’s Guide

🔍 Overview

Valorant is a 5v5 tactical shooter developed by Riot Games, blending precise gunplay and unique character abilities. Each match consists of 25 rounds — the first team to win 13 rounds wins. You either attack or defend sites with the goal of planting/defusing the spike.

🧠 Agent Roles

Agents are grouped into 4 roles. Here's a basic breakdown:

  • Duelists – Self-sufficient fraggers (e.g. Jett, Reyna, Phoenix)
  • Initiators – Start engagements & gather intel (e.g. Sova, Skye)
  • Sentinels – Lock down sites & support (e.g. Sage, Killjoy)
  • Controllers – Manipulate vision & space (e.g. Viper, Omen)

💡 Beginner Tips

  • Practice crosshair placement (aim for the head).
  • Use headphones — audio cues are critical.
  • Use abilities to support your team, not just to get kills.
  • Don’t buy every round. Learn eco and full-buy rounds.
  • Play Deathmatch mode to warm up before ranked games.
